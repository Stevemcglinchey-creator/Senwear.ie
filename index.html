SENwear

Supporting communication in SEN teams — when it matters most

What is SENwear?

SENwear is a lightweight communication tool designed for Special Education settings. It allows key staff to share short, time-relevant alerts so that everyone supporting a student has the same essential information — without relying on WhatsApp or verbal handovers.

The Problem It Solves

  • Critical information missed by substitute or rotating staff
  • Over-reliance on informal messaging apps
  • Repeated strategies without awareness (movement breaks, allowances, priorities)
  • Health, behaviour, or attendance updates not reaching all relevant staff

How the Pilot Works

  • Staff send short alerts (health, attendance, learning, need-to-know)
  • Alerts are visible for 24 hours only
  • Alerts can be acknowledged by relevant roles
  • No student personal data is stored
  • Designed for SENCOs, SNAs, Teachers, Year Heads, and Management
